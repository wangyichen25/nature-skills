# Clinical Deck Patterns

## Slide archetypes

- PICO title slide.
- Study design schematic.
- Eligibility and baseline population slide.
- Primary endpoint slide with absolute and relative effects.
- Harms and safety slide.
- Bias and limitations slide.
- Practice implication slide.
- Appendix with reporting checklist and extra methods.

## Reporting guideline routing

- Trial: CONSORT.
- Observational: STROBE.
- Diagnostic: STARD.
- Prediction/AI: TRIPOD+AI.
- Systematic review: PRISMA.
- AI intervention trial: CONSORT-AI.

## Design defaults

- Avoid marketing-style hero slides.
- Favor readable clinical tables, forest plots, flow diagrams, and endpoint charts.
- Keep one clinical takeaway per slide.
