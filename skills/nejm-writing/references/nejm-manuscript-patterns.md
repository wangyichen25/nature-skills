# Clinical Manuscript Patterns

## Reporting guideline routing

- Randomized trial: CONSORT.
- Trial protocol: SPIRIT.
- Observational: STROBE.
- Diagnostic accuracy: STARD.
- Prediction model or AI prediction: TRIPOD+AI.
- Systematic review/meta-analysis: PRISMA.
- AI intervention trial: CONSORT-AI and SPIRIT-AI.

## Claim boundaries

- Treatment trial: may support efficacy/safety within tested population and follow-up.
- Observational association: supports association, risk marker, or hypothesis, not causality by default.
- Diagnostic study: supports test accuracy in the evaluated setting, not improved outcomes.
- Prediction model: supports performance and possible utility only with validation and decision analysis.
- Workflow AI: supports workflow effect only unless patient outcomes were measured.
