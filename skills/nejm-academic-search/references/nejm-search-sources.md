# Clinical Search Sources

Use these as source classes, not frozen claims. Verify current pages when product status, policy, guideline, or journal requirements matter.

## Core sources

- ICMJE Recommendations: https://www.icmje.org/recommendations
- EQUATOR reporting guidelines: https://www.equator-network.org/reporting-guidelines/
- CONSORT/SPIRIT: https://www.consort-spirit.org/
- ClinicalTrials.gov reporting and registry material: https://clinicaltrials.gov/policy/reporting-requirements
- FDA medical devices and AI/ML pages when regulatory status matters.
- PubMed/MEDLINE for biomedical literature.

## Reporting guideline routing

- Randomized trials: CONSORT; SPIRIT for protocols.
- Observational studies: STROBE.
- Diagnostic accuracy: STARD.
- Systematic reviews/meta-analyses: PRISMA.
- Prediction models: TRIPOD+AI when AI or ML is involved.
- AI intervention trials: CONSORT-AI and SPIRIT-AI.
- Case reports: CARE.
- Quality improvement: SQUIRE.
