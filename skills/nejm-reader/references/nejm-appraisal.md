# Clinical Appraisal

## Study-type checks

- Trial: randomization, allocation concealment, blinding, follow-up, protocol/SAP, primary endpoint, intention-to-treat, harms.
- Observational: cohort selection, confounding, immortal time, measurement bias, missingness, sensitivity analyses.
- Diagnostic: spectrum, reference standard, thresholds, sensitivity/specificity, predictive values, calibration if risk model.
- Prediction/AI: TRIPOD+AI, external validation, calibration, intended use, decision thresholds, drift, subgroup performance.
- Review: PRISMA, search completeness, risk of bias, heterogeneity, certainty of evidence.

## Clinical interpretation

Always distinguish statistical significance, effect size, clinical importance, and implementation feasibility.
