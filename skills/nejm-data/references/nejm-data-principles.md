# Clinical Data Principles

## Source basis

- ICMJE Recommendations for publication ethics, clinical trial registration, data sharing, and authorship.
- ClinicalTrials.gov for trial registration and results-reporting context.
- Journal instructions from NEJM, JAMA, The Lancet, BMJ, and target GI journals.
- EQUATOR reporting guidelines for study-type-specific transparency.

## Required distinctions

- Trial registry record is not a substitute for dataset access.
- Protocol and statistical analysis plan are separate from participant-level data.
- Deidentified data may still be restricted by consent, IRB, data-use agreement, HIPAA, GDPR, or institutional policy.
- Model code, model weights, training data, and validation data are separate artifacts.

## Avoid

- "Available on reasonable request" without eligibility, review route, contact, data-use agreement, and permitted use.
- Claiming public availability when only aggregate tables are public.
- Promising individual participant data sharing when consent or governance does not allow it.
